Swimmers should arrive at least ten minutes early and be ready to get in the water when practice begins.

Early morning option for camp attendees

We offer an extra-early camp practice time for a small number of 7 – 11 year olds that need to go to another camp each day after swim practice. This group meets June 18 – July 10 from 7:00 – 8:00am. From May 27 – June 16, these swimmers will be with their regular age group.

These swimmers must be capable of swimming 25 meters backstroke and freestyle to participate in this practice group.

All-stars

Swimmers who qualify for CSL All-Stars will practice July 20 – 24, 8:00 – 9:30am at the Woods Pool.

Attendance

We encourage swimmers to attend as many practices and meets as possible. The best way to improve is practice. However, we understand that many swimmers will miss some time during the season. We really want your swimmer on the team even if they will miss some of the season!
