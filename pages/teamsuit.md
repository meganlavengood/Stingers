## Suit Fit Guidelines

- **Try on your suit to ensure proper fit.** Sizing varies between brands. 
- Suits should fit **snug** with **no wrinkles or gaps.** 
- Jammers should end **above the knee**.
- New suits feel tight at first - they loosen when wet and with wear.
- **Try your suit on _before_ getting in the water.**
- Suits **cannot** be returned or exchanged once worn in the water.
