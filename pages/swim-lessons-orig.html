Stingers coaches will offer semi-private lessons (2 swimmers per lesson), at several pools during the afternoons. Lessons are $20/swimmer for 30 minutes, payable directly to the coach.

When signing up for morning lessons, please keep the following information in mind:

1. Lessons are semi-private. There will typically be 2 swimmers per coach. Lesson sign-ups will be in coaches lesson books which are found outside the gates of the pool during practice. Lesson sign-ups are first come, first choice. Be sure to include your swimmer's name, age, and need in the Optional Contact Info block when you sign up. This will help your coach plan more effectively for the lesson.

2.  You may sign up only one swimmer per spot. If you want to have siblings lesson together, that would be two spots, not two swimmers in one spot.

3. If you need to cancel, please do so directly with the coach so another family may take the spot. You can also email Coach James at james@machineacquatics.com. Coaches must be given >24 hour cancellation notification or you may be charged a cancellation fee. You can find their contact information in their lessons book.

4. Coaches reserve the right to re-assign swimmers based on swimmer needs in order to better serve the swimmers (ie pairing backstroke lesson with a backstroke lesson).
