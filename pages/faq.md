# Frequently Asked Questions

## Does my family need to have a pool membership?

Yes, you do need to have a [Burke Centre Conservancy Pool Membership](https://engage.goenumerate.com/s/burkecentre/hoapage.php?page=gen_7_7293) to join the Stingers. Pool membership includes access to all five pools in the community.

## Does my swimmer need to be evaluated? How do swimmer evaluations work?

Yes, new swimmer evaluations occur each year before the season begins. You can find more information on our [Join](https://www.gomotionapp.com/team/reccobcs/page/join) page.

## Can I reserve a practice time prior to registration for my swimmer?

No, all practice times are first come, first served upon opening of registration. 

## Can my mini swimmer switch practice times? 

At the time of registration, you are committing to a practice slot for the season. Please plan to honor this commitment throughout the season.

As the season progresses, there may be opportunities to change practice times as minis move up to the lanes for practice, but do not rely on these opportunities to change the practice time for your mini.

## Do I have to volunteer for my child to swim with the Stingers?

The Stingers have a mandatory volunteer requirement. Over the course of the season all families must either 1) volunteer for a minimum of 6 volunteer shifts, or 2) pay a volunteer fee of $300.

We collect volunteer deposit checks during the first week of practice. Your check will only be cashed if you do not meet the volunteer requirement. Check out our [Volunteer handbook and job descriptions](https://www.gomotionapp.com/reccobcs/UserFiles/Image/QuickUpload/2023-volunteer-policy-and-job-descriptions-1-1-_026870.pdf) for more information.

## I really want to volunteer, but I am not able to make it to meets or social events. What can I do?

There are so many opportunities to take advantage of during the season to help your family meet the volunteer requirement that will work with your schedule. Some examples include:

- hauling equipment to and from events
- providing items for the snack bar
- cleaning up the pool deck and locker rooms during and after practice

…and other behind the scenes positions.

Please reach out to the Stinger's volunteer coordinator with any questions.

## How do I apply to be a coach?

We accept applications for coaches at two levels. Assistant and Junior coaches must be on the Burke Centre Stingers swim team roster to be considered for employment.

**Junior Coaches** will be part-time and new to coaching, with a focus on learning how to coach (previously designated as an intern). Junior Coaches must be eligible for employment and at least 15 years old.

**Assistant Coaches** are returning full-time coaches, or coaches with experience.

[Apply as a Junior or Assistant coach](https://forms.gle/cRvQJA39SksSS8z26)

We will reach out about interviews shortly after receiving your application. If you have any questions please email [stingerspresident@gmail.com](mailto:stingerspresident@gmail.com).
