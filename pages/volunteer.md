# Volunteer

We rely heavily on our families to help run meets and social events.  Please consider it a fun way to get involved and make new friends on deck.

## Volunteer requirement

The Stingers have a mandatory volunteer requirement. 

- All families must volunteer for their team for a minimum of 6 volunteer shifts.
- Families who do not volunteer must pay an additional fee of $300.

We collect volunteer fee deposit checks during the first week of practice (made payable to Burke Centre Stingers for $300). Once your volunteer requirement is fulfilled, the check is shredded.

Check out our [Volunteer handbook and job descriptions](/reccobcs/UserFiles/Image/QuickUpload/2023-volunteer-policy-and-job-descriptions-1-1-_026870.pdf) for more information.

**Please reach out to Stinger's volunteer coordinator with any questions.  We have so many ways that you can help that will work with your schedule.**
