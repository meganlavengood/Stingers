Welcome to the Burke Centre Stingers! Our team is a summer developmental and competitive swim team that includes year-round opportunities offering exceptional coaching and tons of fun for all ages and abilities! Kids enjoy the camaraderie of a team sport, gain self-confidence as they develop a life skill, get plenty of physical activity, and have tons of fun time with friends during team events.  

You don't need to be a Burke Centre resident to join our team, but you do need to have a Burke Centre Conservancy Pool Membership. Check out the Burke Centre Conservancy website for details about purchasing your pool membership. Pool membership includes access to FIVE pools in the community.

The Burke Centre Stingers, part of the Burke Centre Swim Club, competes in the Colonial Swim League (CSL).

Our competition pool is The Commons Pool @ 5701 Roberts Parkway Burke, VA 22015.
