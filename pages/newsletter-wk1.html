Time Trials

Stingers Time Trials will be held this Saturday, June 6th at the Commons Pool. 10 and under swimmers should arrive by 7:00 AM, and 11 and over swimmers should arrive by 7:20 AM. Warm ups begin at 7:10 AM for 10 and under swimmers, 7:30 AM for 11 and over swimmers, and the meet will begin promptly at 8:00 AM. The declaration deadline is Thursday, June 4th at 11:59 PM, and registration will be open shortly. If your child plans to swim at time trials please declare your swimmer "yes" and then pick which strokes they are swimming. If you do not plan to swim, please declare your swimmer "no".

Your swimmer can swim up to five events if they are approved by the coach. Minis must get approval from a coach to swim Freestyle in Time Trials. Coach James will be conducting stroke evaluations at practice throughout the week. If you have a new 10 & under (who is not a mini) and are unsure of what to declare for, please only declare your swimmer for freestyle. A coach will notify you if your swimmer is able to do more strokes, or if they aren't ready for a meet.

Please purchase your Burke Centre pool membership ASAP if you have not already. The BCC makes sure everyone on the roster has a pool pass. If you do not have one by June 3rd we will have to remove your child from the roster without a refund - no exceptions. Instructions and more information can be found on the Conservancy's website.

Volunteer Deposit Check

All families must provide a $300 check made out to Burke Centre Stingers before Wednesday, June 3rd as a volunteer hold. Once you have reached your required 6 volunteer points, we will shred your check. Your swimmer will not be able to sign up for events (including Time Trials) until the check is provided. Please give your volunteer deposit check to Molly Guthrie or Lora Allen (volunteer coordinators), or check with any of the Board members during evening practices.

Parking

As we all know, parking is limited at the Commons. If there are no parking spaces available please park at the VRE parking lot. You could get a ticket if you park on Premier Court or make your own spot in the Commons parking lot. Please also do not park in the handicap parking spaces if you do not need that space.
