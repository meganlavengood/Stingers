# 2026 Stingers Practice Schedule

Here is our 2026 Stinger Practice Schedule. We encourage swimmers to attend as many practices and meets as possible. The best way to improve is practice. However, we understand that many swimmers will miss some time during the season. We really want your swimmer on the team even if they will miss some of the season!

We are bringing back our camp practice time for a small number of 7-11 year olds that need to go to camp each day. These swimmers must be capable of swimming 25 meters backstroke and freestyle to participate in this practice group. We are also again offering overlapping age groups this year to accomodate families with multiple children in different age groups. Please be aware that the 9-12 year old practice time will be similar in intensity/skills to our previous 9-10 year old practice.

*Times may shift depending on registration numbers.

OTHER INFO: Swimmers should arrive at least ten minutes early and be ready to get in the water when practice begins.

MINIS: The Minis are swimmers who cannot swim the length of the pool, and so they practice in the shallow end during practices. Minis are typically 4-6 years old and some 7-8 year old swimmers at the beginning of the season. Minis will choose which group they are assigned at registration. Numbers will be limited for minis groups so please register early to obtain your desired practice time. Minis must be comfortable getting into and out of the water by themselves without parental assistance. They must be comfortable putting their face in the water and be able to follow instructions.
