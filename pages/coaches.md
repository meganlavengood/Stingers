## Head Coach: James Walsh

We are excited to have James return for his 2nd year as Head Coach of the Stingers. James has spent more than a decade in and around pools as a swim coach, instructor, and lifeguard. James is currently a Senior Group and High School Prep lead with Machine Aquatics, and is the Stingers winter swim program head coach.  James is a graduate of George Mason University with a  Masters degree in Digital World History. In his free time,  James organizes and runs a men's futsal team that participates in a league across the east coast.

James is dedicated to coaching and takes great joy in helping swimmers reach their full potential. James is excited to get started with the 2026 summer season!

Our 2026 coaching staff brings a wide range of skills and experience to the team! If you have questions about your swimmer throughout the season, the best place to start is with the age group coaches.
