The Stingers Winter Swim Program is a non-competitive alternative for swimmers wanting to be in the water all year, but not ready or interested in the greater time commitment of a competitive winter swim program. 

The goals are to:

- Become legal in all four strokes
- Develop stroke technique and efficiency
- Refine starts and turns
- Increase conditioning

…while having fun with Stinger friends!

**Head Coach James Walsh** will lead the charge for us again this year, and other Stingers coaches will provide support throughout.

## Requirements

- The Winter Swim Program is open to **lane swimmers only** due to the depth of the pool.
- All swimmers should be able to comfortably swim 25 meters unassisted. 
- **Any interested Minis swimmers will need to have approval** from Coach James prior to registration. 
- **All swimmers must attend 50% or more sessions.** Due to limited lane space, we will have an attendance policy for winter swim. If a swimmer is attending fewer than 50% of sessions and we have swimmers on the waitlist, the family will be notified that their spot is revoked, a prorated refund will be offered, and the spot will be given to a family on the waitlist.

Swimmers who swim multiple days in other year round programs  (Machine, Makos, NCAP, Foxes, SWB, Marlins, etc.) should not sign up for Stingers Winter Swim.

## Location

Both sessions will be held at **[Audrey Moore Rec Center](https://www.fairfaxcounty.gov/parks/reccenter/audrey-moore)**.

## Sessions and levels

The Stingers will offer two sessions this year.

- While there are no official age requirements, if possible, please register according to age:
    - Session 1 will be geared more towards our younger swimmers (12 and under)
    - Session 2 will focus more on older (11 and over) and more advanced swimmers
    - We ask that all 8 and under swimmers sign up for Session 1 if possible.
- **Please only sign up for one session per swimmer.**

## Schedule

- Session 1: Sundays, 6:00–7:00pm
- Session 2: Sundays, 7:00–8:00pm

The program will begin on **September 21st**, and will run for 35 weeks, ending on **May 17th**. 

No-swim dates for this year include:

- November 30th (Thanksgiving)
- December 28th (winter break)
- January 4th (winter break)
- January 18th (MLK)
- April 5th (Easter)

Please also note the attendance policy [above](#requirements).

## Cost 

The cost for the program is **$325 per swimmer** plus Team Unify and credit card processing fees.

Please note:

- Payment is due at the time of registration. 
- **The Stingers have a NO REFUND policy.** If we have swimmers on a waitlist, a prorated refund may be offered in order to assign your slot to another swimmer. No refunds will be offered if there are no swimmers on the waitlist.
