New Swimmer Evaluation

2026 Evaluation: May 26, 2026 5:00-7:00 PM at the Commons Pool

All new Stingers ages 10 and under must join us for a swim evaluation. Swimmers must be able to meet Burke Centre Swim Club performance criteria based on Red Cross Level III standards and must be comfortable in the water. New swimmer evaluations are to ensure safety in the pool and proper practice placement.

Swimmers that were "Minis" in the prior year (i.e., in the shallow end the entire season) that would like to be swimming in lanes this summer should also come to Swimmer Evaluation.

Minis must be comfortable getting into and out of the water by themselves without parental assistance. They must be comfortable putting their face in the water and be able to follow instructions.

In the event that a swimmer does not pass the evaluation, registration fees will be refunded.

Our coaches and volunteers will be there to answer any questions you have.
