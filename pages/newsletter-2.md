## Upcoming meets

The Stingers Blue and White B Meet is this Wednesday, June 10th. 

- Warm ups begin at 5:00 PM. 
- Swimmers are able to sign up for a **maximum of 3 strokes**, and we will do on deck write ins for IMs. Please **do not sign your swimmer up for IMs** as that will count towards their three strokes. 
- Only sign your swimmers up for strokes they have received approval to swim. 
- You must declare your swimmers by **Monday night at 11:59 PM**. 
- The stroke order will be Free, Back, Breast, Fly, and IM (write ins) if time permits. 
- The theme for this meet is **tropical**—wear your favorite Hawaiian shirt or other bright colored gear!

Our first A Meet is scheduled for Saturday, June 13 away at Ashburn Village. 
- The deadline to declare is **Wednesday June 10th at 11:59PM**. 
- You will only indicate if your swimmers will be available to swim that day. Coach James will make the final determination on the A Meet roster. 
- If you are available to swim please declare your swimmer, even if you don't think your swimmer will make the roster. We usually have a few exhibition (non-scoring) heats for our younger swimmers, and we never know who might be unavailable. 
- Stay tuned for A Meet team lunch details. 
- The meet theme for this meet is **twins**—match your outfit with a friend! 

Meet themes can be found on our [upcoming meets](https://www.gomotionapp.com/team/reccobcs/page/current-season/custom-page) page.
