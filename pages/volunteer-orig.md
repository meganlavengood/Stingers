We rely heavily on our families to help run meets and social events.  Please consider it a fun way to get involved and make new friends on deck.

The Stingers have a mandatory volunteer requirement. All families must volunteer a minimum of 6 volunteer shifts during the swim season for their team or pay a volunteer fee of $300. We collect volunteer deposit checks during the first week of practice (made payable to Burke Centre Stingers for $300). Check out our ***[Volunteer handbook and job descriptions](/reccobcs/UserFiles/Image/QuickUpload/2023-volunteer-policy-and-job-descriptions-1-1-_026870.pdf)*** for more information.

**_Please reach out to Stinger's volunteer coordinator with any questions.  We have so many ways that you can help that will work with your schedule. _**

Each year the Burke Centre Swim Club recognizes volunteers who have embodied the spirit of tirelessly volunteering for the team and specifically for demonstrating significant contributions to the total outcome of the success of the swim program.

The Myron C. Lynch Award is given in honor of Myron C. Lynch Jr, a Burke Centre Resident and active member of the Burke Centre Swim Club. Myron C. Lynch was a devoted husband to Naomi and father to his daughters, Miriam and Sarah, who both swam with the Penguins. In addition to his leadership with the Burke Centre Swim Club, Myron also helped to coach his daughter's sports teams, was a volunteer with the Potomac Valley Swimming Association, and served as a swim official for the Black History Invitational Swim Meet and the Burke Special Olympics.
